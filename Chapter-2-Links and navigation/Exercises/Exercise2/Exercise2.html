The Blacklist

The Blacklist is a crime drama TV series that follows Raymond "Red" Reddington, a former criminal mastermind who mysteriously surrenders to the FBI and offers to help capture high-profile criminals on his "Blacklist." However, his assistance comes with one condition: he will only work with rookie profiler Elizabeth Keen.
